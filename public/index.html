Trove

Trove is a New York City-based theatre and live arts collective. Trove is a rejection of the scarcity mindset in favor of lightness, community, and the belief that we already have everything we need. Trove is having fun while making radical, delightful, really good art with and for the people we love. Trove forever!
Our next project is The Last Annual Spelling Bee, October 22 at The Tank. It’s not theatre, it’s not a gimmick, it’s just a spelling bee

Treasures
Treasures are full-length, public performances, offering space for artists to present their work.
No theatre. No gimmicks. Just a spelling bee. Twenty-five people competing for fame, glory, and a big doughnut, cheered on by their friends, family, and peers. We care about meaning, but tonight we care about spelling.
Trinkets
Trinkets are short, generative workshops led by an artist who wants to open up their creative process to others.
